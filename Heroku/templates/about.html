About the proyect

The objective of the project consisted in the development of a platform dedicated to natural language processing. The platform will allow entering comments to obtain with great confidence whether they are positive or negative comments, analysis of sentences, etc.

The development will be done in Python using the Flask microframework, the nltk library with multiple text corpus and the Stanford text analyzer.
